Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad is a public university, located in Hyderabad, Telangana.
Founded in 1965 as the Nagarjuna Sagar Engineering College, it was established as a university in 1972 by The Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Act, 1972.
The university is situated at Kukatpally Housing Board Region in Hyderabad of India.
